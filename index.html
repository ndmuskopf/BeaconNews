Local Man Allegedly Tampered With Home Spa Water Tanks

Authorities in McCracken county of Kentucky say a hobbyist technician is under investigation for unconventional “repairs” to discounted home spa units.

By Casey Rowland
Aug 28, 2025
5 min read

Paducah, Kentucky — In a case that investigators insist is as unusual for their small town, a local college student is said to have ejaculated into the water tanks of several home sauna units and returned them to the store, to be resold and purchased from discount auction sites similar to BidFTA.

Officials in McCracken County Department of Public Wellness reportedly received a handful of complaints about off putting “additives” in recently acquired spa devices. The alleged source was described only as a “self appointed sauna specialist,” whose methods were deemed “deeply misguided” by authorities.

According to a spokesperson for the agency, the devices were then sold through generic warehouse liquidation outlets that resell customer returns and overstock.

“This is undoubtably the most foul case I have ever worked,” the spokesperson said. “Any leads on this disgusting man need to be reported to the police as soon as possible, so that we can deal with this matter swiftly.”

How the Investigation Began

The tale began when several buyers of discount home spa units noticed unusual odors after filling the tanks. The suspect allegedly believed the addition of semen would provide “natural enhancements.”

Public Guidance

Buying Refurbished Gear

Inspect units thoroughly, replace filters, and sanitize tanks according to manufacturer instructions.

Hygiene First

Use approved disinfectants and stick to safe, non biological additives. When in doubt, contact the manufacturer.

Know Your Source

Purchase from reputable outlets and verify return policies and warranties.

As of publication, the individual has not been named, because no such person has been found.
